These are the Top Music

1.Butter-BTS “Butter” is a dance-pop song with lyrical confessions of sweet, romantic love. The fun and witty lyrics brimming with positive and bright energy will instantly uplift moods. The track uniquely shows each of the BTS' member’s charms as they show how smooth and at times charismatic, they can be.

2.good 4 u-olivia rodrigo " Good 4 U " (stylized in all lowercase) is the third single and sixth track from Olivia Rodrigo 's debut studio album, SOUR. It was released on May 14th, 2021 by Geffen Records . Rodrigo announced her debut studio album Sour on April 1, 2021. "Good 4 U" placed sixth on the track listing.

3.levatating-dua lipa Rocking a '70s-inspired Valentino Haute Couture tasseled sequin gown, Dua Lipa gave the first televised performance of "Levitating" on the November 20, 2020 episode of BBC's The Graham Norton Show. She sang the song again two days later at the 2020 American Music Awards. Filmed at London's Royal Albert Hall in a custom-built set, Dua Lipa ended her performance by levitating towards the sky.

4.stay-the kid laroi “Stay” is a collaboration between The Kid LAROI and Justin Bieber which details LAROI’s wish for his lover’s forgiveness and Bieber’s admiration for his significant other. The song was first previewed on LAROI’s Instagram story in September 2020 and the following month in a Rolling Loud-conducted Twitch interview hosted by DJ Scheme.

5.bad habits-ed sheeran This moody song finds Ed Sheeran in a deep internal conflict about his bad habits. His unhealthy lifestyle, such as nighttime drinking and partying, is leading him to make some poor choices, which he later regrets.
